## Writing Style

**Format:**
- 150-300 words per post (short, punchy essays)
- 3-5 short paragraphs
- Opens with a hook or counterintuitive observation
- Builds reasoning through concrete examples
- Closes with a memorable takeaway, call-to-action, or thought-provoking question

**Tone:**
- Direct and philosophical
- Action-oriented with practical observations
- Conversational, accessible language (no jargon)
- Uses paradox or inversion as rhetorical devices
- Repeats key phrases for emphasis

**Structure:**
- Counterintuitive observation → Mechanical explanation → Practical implication
- Focus on hidden costs/benefits or inverted perspectives

## What Makes a Good Post Idea

1. **Counterintuitive angle** - Challenges conventional wisdom
2. **Mechanical insight** - Explains the "why" behind behavior/outcomes
3. **Actionable** - Reader can apply it immediately
4. **Memorable** - Has a quotable core phrase
5. **Original** - Not covered in recent posts
6. **Fits the theme** - Relates to sovereignty, asymmetry, or clear thinking
